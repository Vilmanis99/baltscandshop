Archive Shelving (Tubular)

PROSPACE+ Archive Shelf

Tubular shelving configured for archive and document storage. 7 shelf levels at 2,200 mm height with 350 mm depth optimised for standard archive boxes. Available in single-access (350 mm) or double-access (700 mm) depth. Clean aesthetic with no front-facing perforations, suitable for offices, hospitals and administrative environments.

  • Load capacity up to 2,100 kg per pair of uprights
  • Height up to 5,000 mm in one piece
  • Adjustment pitch 40 mm
  • Depth up to 1,000 mm
  • Load up to 275 kg per shelf (evenly distributed)
  • Clip-in assembly, no tools required
  • Guaranteed European origin
