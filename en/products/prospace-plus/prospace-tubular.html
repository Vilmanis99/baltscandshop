Tubular Shelving

PROSPACE+ Tubular Shelf

Tubular shelves with front beams, side beams and inner tubes. Directly attached to uprights. Available with hardboard, galvanised sheet or mesh cover.

  • Load capacity up to 2,100 kg per pair of uprights
  • Height up to 5,000 mm in one piece
  • Adjustment pitch 40 mm
  • Depth up to 1,000 mm
  • Load up to 275 kg per shelf (evenly distributed)
  • Clip-in assembly, no tools required
  • Guaranteed European origin

Upright Types

Type A1,400 kg (1.4 t) per pair
Type C2,100 kg (2.1 t) per pair
  • Non-perforated front, 0.8 or 1.2 mm steel
  • Continuous cold-rolled, 10 folds, Ω-shaped
  • Standard finishes: grey epoxy RAL 7016 or galvanised
  • Adjustment pitch 40 mm
  • No mounting direction
