PROPLUS LP3 Pallet Safety Gates

Tilt-up and folding pallet safety gates for safe loading and unloading on mezzanine platforms. Prevents falling objects and maintains worker safety when handling palletised goods at height. Two gate types available for regular and intensive use.

  • Maximum span up to 10 metres
  • Up to 850 kg/m2 load capacity
  • EU-certified platform, Eurocode 3
  • 3 flooring types: wood, grating, sheet metal
  • Fully modular — add/reduce square metres
  • Bolted assembly with 50 mm pitch
  • Alternative to construction without extra rent
