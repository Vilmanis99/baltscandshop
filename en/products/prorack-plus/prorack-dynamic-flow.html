Dynamic Racking

PRORACK+ Dynamic Flow Racking

FIFO dynamic racking with roller conveyors integrated into PRORACK+ frames. Cartons loaded at rear flow by gravity to picking face. Ideal for order picking, e-commerce fulfilment and high-throughput warehouses.

  • Load from 150 to 1,200 kg per level
  • Height up to 6,000 mm in one piece
  • Tested per European standard FEM 10.2.02
  • 12 depths from 400 to 1,500 mm
  • 15 heights from 1,800 to 6,000 mm
  • Adjustment range 50 mm
  • 100% Sendzimir galvanised steel frames
