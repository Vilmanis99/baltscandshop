Garment Storage

PRORACK+ Garment Rack

Garment hanging configuration using PRORACK+ frames with horizontal hanging rails. For clothing storage in retail, laundry, uniform management and textile warehouses. Can combine upper shelf levels for boxed items above hanging garments.

  • Load from 150 to 1,200 kg per level
  • Height up to 6,000 mm in one piece
  • Tested per European standard FEM 10.2.02
  • 12 depths from 400 to 1,500 mm
  • 15 heights from 1,800 to 6,000 mm
  • Adjustment range 50 mm
  • 100% Sendzimir galvanised steel frames
