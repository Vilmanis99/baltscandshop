MODUL+ Panels

Frame made of 20x20 mm square tubes with welded wire mesh 50x50 mm. 7 heights (1,800–3,000 mm), 4 widths (800–1,500 mm). Wire mesh, sheet metal, half mesh, or 5S transparent cladding.

  • Indoor or outdoor use (galvanised version)
  • Height up to 6,000 mm
  • Standard colours: Blue RAL 5019, Grey RAL 7016
  • Fully adaptable modular system
  • Security brackets — cannot be dismantled from outside
  • Hinged or sliding doors
  • Custom installation service available
