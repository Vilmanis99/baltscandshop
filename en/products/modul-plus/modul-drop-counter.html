MODUL+ Drop Counter & Partitioning Options

Versatile partitioning add-ons including drop counter panels for passing small items without opening the main door, lockable steel shelves, and various closing options. Enhances functionality of any MODUL+ installation.

  • Indoor or outdoor use (galvanised version)
  • Height up to 6,000 mm
  • Standard colours: Blue RAL 5019, Grey RAL 7016
  • Fully adaptable modular system
  • Security brackets — cannot be dismantled from outside
  • Hinged or sliding doors
  • Custom installation service available
