About Baltscand

Baltscand is the official Provost distributor for Finland and the Baltic states. We bring European-quality industrial storage solutions with local expertise and service.

About Provost

Founded in 1963 in Halluin, France, Provost has grown into one of Europe's leading manufacturers of industrial shelving and storage solutions. With over 800 employees, 6 European factories, and 80% in-house manufacturing, Provost delivers quality and reliability across 10,000+ product references.
