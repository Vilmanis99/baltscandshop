PROSPACE+ Workshop & Store Room

Complete workshop and store room storage solutions using PROSPACE+ modular shelving. Purpose-configured for tools, parts, supplies and equipment in workshops, maintenance areas and back-of-store rooms. Combines tubular and metal sheet shelves for mixed-load storage.

  • Load capacity up to 2,100 kg per pair of uprights
  • Height up to 5,000 mm in one piece
  • Adjustment pitch 40 mm
  • Depth up to 1,000 mm
  • Load up to 275 kg per shelf (evenly distributed)
  • Clip-in assembly, no tools required
  • Guaranteed European origin
