PROPLUS LP3 Order Picking Platform

Tailor-made mezzanine platforms optimised for order picking operations. Multi-level configurations double or triple floor space for efficient pick-pack-ship workflows. Available with integrated conveyors, special colours and custom layouts.

  • Maximum span up to 10 metres
  • Up to 850 kg/m2 load capacity
  • EU-certified platform, Eurocode 3
  • 3 flooring types: wood, grating, sheet metal
  • Fully modular — add/reduce square metres
  • Bolted assembly with 50 mm pitch
  • Alternative to construction without extra rent
